The World of Ciretose

Ciretose is a living world shaped by ancient magical forces. Every species has evolved under arcane influence, leading to magical divergence like Shadow, Celestial, and Primal mutations. The setting explores ecological, political, and mythic depth.

  • Evolutionary bestiary with magical variants
  • Leyline-based arcane geography
  • Historical magical epochs
  • Political intrigue alongside cosmic conflict
