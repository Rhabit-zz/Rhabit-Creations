The World of Ciretose

The world of Ciretose began as the setting for my table top campaigns and over many years turned into an entire source book of information. It is a living Earth sized world with hundreds of regions shaped by ancient energies and cycles.

Every species has evolved under arcane influence, leading to magically enhanced evolutions. The setting explores ecological, political, and mythic depth.
