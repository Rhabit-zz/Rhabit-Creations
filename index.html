Welcome

My name is Brandon Lirette, nickname RHabit (Repetitive Habits). I design systems and worlds with immersive mechanics, tactical combat systems, and world-scale lore rooted in ecosystem logic and magical divergence.

My philosophy:

Game Design: Balance is key. If the challenge is too low the game is boring. Too high: frustrating. Also, don't tell players what to be, instead provide options with a lot of room for expansion, especially for people like myself who are willing to put in a bit of work to create their own vision. I prefer building blocks over pre-built development paths. That said, I also understand not every player wants to build everything, so I also like to produce usable ready to play examples.

World building: People need something they can relate to in order to be immersive, and diversity is a key pillar. Worlds evolve around character choices and documenting a world is an extensive process that provides as many options as possible while avoiding railroading.
