Unbound RPG System

Currently developing a modular tabletop RPG system focused on tactical depth, evolutionary bestiary design, and meaningful player choice. Designed to address mechanical stagnation and narrative inflexibility in existing systems like 5E.

  • Modular class tree system
  • AP-based tactical combat
  • XP-based progression with tiered skills
  • Custom status effects and conditions
  • AI-integrated prototyping tools
